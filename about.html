🏫 About CGP

We're on a Mission to Simplify College Discovery

CGP was founded with one belief — every student deserves the right information to make the most important decision of their life.

👋 Who We Are

Built by Students, For Students

College Group Point (CGP) was born out of frustration. Our founders went through the stressful process of finding the right college — and realized that most students were navigating this journey completely alone, with confusing or outdated information.

So we built CGP: a simple, beautiful, student-first platform that centralizes college discovery, course information, and career guidance — all in one place. Today, we help thousands of students across India and worldwide find their perfect college.
