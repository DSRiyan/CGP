❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about CGP? We've got answers.

How does CGP help students find colleges?
▼

CGP uses a step-by-step College Finder system where you tell us your education level, preferred location, stream, and subject interests. Based on your answers, we instantly recommend colleges that best match your profile — from top Indian institutions to global universities.

Is CGP free to use?
▼

Yes! CGP is 100% free for all students. There are no subscription plans, no hidden charges, and no premium tiers. Every feature — from the College Finder to our guides — is completely accessible at no cost.

Can I find international universities on CGP?
▼

Absolutely. CGP covers universities across 50+ countries worldwide, including top institutions in the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Germany, and many more. Simply choose "Worldwide" in the location step of our College Finder to see international recommendations.

Do you help with course selection?
▼

Yes! One of our core services is Course Guidance. We help you understand which courses align with your stream, interests, and long-term career goals. Whether you're choosing between B.Sc. and B.Tech, or MBA vs. MCA — we provide clear comparisons and guidance.

How do I contact CGP for support?
▼

You can reach us at [email protected] or call us at +91 12345 67890 during business hours (Mon–Sat, 9 AM to 6 PM IST). We typically respond to all email queries within 24 hours.

What education levels does CGP cover?
▼

CGP covers all major education levels — Junior College (Class 11–12), Bachelor's Degree programs, Master's Degree programs, and Professional Courses. Whether you're just finishing Class 10 or planning a postgraduate degree, CGP has resources for you.

Is my personal data safe with CGP?
▼

Yes, your privacy is our top priority. We only collect the minimum information needed to personalize your experience. We never sell your data to third parties. Your information is stored securely and you can request deletion at any time by contacting us.
